Numerical data

The numerical data for the soft matters are generated from numerical simulations in Finite Element Method (FEM). Our data are generated by Qi Yao via COMSOL, a FEM software.

Geospatial data

We thank NSF award achiver for providing us records of recent fundings given to soft matters labs. We also thank OpenStreetMap and Mapbox for providing georeference data (coordinates) of each lab. The shapefile of the United States are archived from Esri ArcGIS Online resource with a personal license from Shan Ye.
